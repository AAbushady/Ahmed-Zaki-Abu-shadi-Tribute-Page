Ahmed Zaki Abu Shadi

Poet | Publisher | Doctor | Scientist

Some facts about Dr. Abu Shadi

  • 1892: Born on Februaary 9th in Cairo, Egypt.
  • 1912: Moves to London, England.
  • 1917: Graduated from University of London,
    with distinction; where he studied medicine.
  • 1919: Founded the Apis Club. As well as patenting
    a standardized aluminum honeycomb.
  • 1920: Married Anna Bamford.
  • 1922: Returns to Egypt.
  • 1930: Established "The Bee Kingdom League".
  • 1932: Founded the poetry journal "Apollo".
  • 1955: Passed away at age 63.
  • 2019: His Great-Grandson creates this webpage.
